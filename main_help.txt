Spores Game Commands
====================

help:
    Prints the help menu.
clear:
    Clears the terminal screen.
harvest:
    Harvests spores and adds them to your total.
info:
    Prints your current spores, harvest stats, and farm name.
exit:
    Exits the game after saving.
load:
    Loads your game data.
save:
    Saves your game data.
new game:
    Starts a new game (asks for confirmation).
license:
    Prints the license information for the game.
